Why Preventive Dentistry Matters for Kids

Frequently Asked Questions

When should my child start seeing a dentist?

By age one or within six months of their first tooth appearing.

Are fluoride treatments safe?

Yes. They are highly effective and safe for kids.

How often are cleanings needed?

Every 6 months unless more frequent visits are advised.

What are dental sealants?

A protective layer applied to molars to prevent cavities.

My child is scared of the dentist. What should I do?

We specialize in gentle, fun, fear-reducing techniques tailored for kids.
